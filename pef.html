Punjab Education Foundation (PEF)

Under the direct ministerial oversight of Rana Sikandar Hayat, Provincial Minister of School & Higher Education, PEF delivers free quality education to 2.5 million+ underprivileged children across all 36 districts of Punjab through innovative public-private partnerships and state-of-the-art educational initiatives. Established in 1991 as an autonomous statutory body, PEF has become Pakistan's most successful education flagship program.

What is PEF?

About Punjab Education Foundation

"PEF exists to ensure that not a single child in Punjab is denied an education due to poverty. Free quality schooling — at every child's doorstep."

— PEF Mission, under Ministerial Oversight of Rana Sikandar Hayat

The Punjab Education Foundation (PEF) was established in 1991 as an autonomous statutory body under the Punjab Education Foundation Act. Today, it operates as a flagship public-private partnership model overseen by the Ministry of School Education, Punjab — headed by Rana Sikandar Hayat, Provincial Minister of School & Higher Education.

Programs & Initiatives

PEF's Core Programs & Models

PEF operates through multiple innovative models, each designed to reach different communities and ensure no child is denied free quality education due to poverty.

🏫
FAS Program

Foundation Assisted Schools

PEF partners with existing private low-cost schools, providing per-child stipends that enable completely free education. All partner schools undergo rigorous quality testing through regular Quality Assurance Tests (QAT).

📊 Largest by enrollment
🎟️
EVS Scheme

Education Voucher Scheme

Eligible families in urban slum areas receive educational vouchers that allow them to enroll children at any partner school of their choice. The voucher covers the complete cost of schooling.

✅ Family empowerment & choice
🌱
NSP Initiative

New School Program

PEF invites entrepreneurs to establish brand-new schools in underserved areas with no existing private school partner. A one-time infrastructure grant helps establish the school on quality foundations.

📍 Expanding to unserved areas
🏆
NSSE Initiative

Nawaz Sharif Schools of Eminence

Launched in March 2026 under Rana Sikandar Hayat's oversight. 155 state-of-the-art schools with science labs, computer labs, robotics labs, STEAM labs, and ECCE classrooms — all offering free education.

⭐ 155 Schools · Gold Standard
📊
CPDP Program

Continuous Professional Development

PEF's quality directorate runs ongoing teacher training, certification programs, and capacity-building workshops — ensuring partner school teachers stay current with modern pedagogy and best practices.

🎓 Teacher excellence at scale
🔍
QAT System

Quality Assurance Tests

PEF conducts rigorous Quality Assurance Tests at regular intervals across partner schools. Schools are categorized A+, A, B, C based on performance — incentivizing quality and accountability across the entire network.

🏅 A+ = Gold Standard

How It Works

Nawaz Sharif Schools Framework

1️⃣

MoU Signing

PEF signs agreements with entrepreneurs, NGOs, EdTech firms, and educational chains to establish schools.

2️⃣

Infrastructure (45 Days)

School management develops modern infrastructure to PEF's prescribed standards for quality education.

3️⃣

PEF Inspection

PEF inspects completed schools against rigorous quality criteria before granting enrollment permission.

4️⃣

Free Education Begins

Students receive entirely free education — the Punjab Government covers all tuition fees completely.

More Milestones

World Bank Recognition — AI in Education

The World Bank officially recognized Punjab's AI-Powered Learning Initiative as a model for educational technology adoption in developing regions. Punjab's implementation of Google Gemini and Read Along technology across government schools drew praise for its scale, execution quality, and measurable literacy outcomes — making Punjab one of the first provincial governments in South Asia to achieve World Bank-level endorsement for EdTech integration.

Digital Safer Future MoU — 1,000 Teachers, 50,000 Students

Signed the Digital Safer Future partnership with PITB and Tech Valley to train 1,000 STEM teachers, who would then deliver online safety, basic coding, and digital literacy skills to 50,000 students in grades 6 to 8 across Punjab.

PITB · May 9, 2024
🏫

PEF Expansion Review — 2.6M Learners in Partner Schools

Reviewed Punjab Education Foundation operations and backed the expansion of its public-private partnership model, which was already delivering free education to more than 2.6 million students through over 7,000 partner schools across the province.

Pakistan Today · March 12, 2024

272 Board Toppers Honoured with Scholarships & Cash Awards

Free Education for Balochistan Girls — UHE Syndicate Decision

Expanded the school meal program to provide nutritious meals to over 75,000 students daily across Punjab's government schools. 2025

Ghost Enrollment Elimination Drive

Led a landmark transparency and accountability drive that eliminated nearly two million ghost enrollments from Punjab's education database. Simultaneously rationalized 26,000 teachers across the province to address real staffing gaps — removing redundancies and placing qualified teachers in schools where they are actually needed. This reform is estimated to save substantial public funds annually and improve per-child resource allocation.

💰 Impact:2M+ Ghost Records Removed · 26K Teachers Rationalized · Billions Saved
Transparency Reform Accountability Fiscal Efficiency

This landmark initiative bridged the public-private digital divide, ensuring merit is rewarded with the tools necessary to excel in a technology-driven world — regardless of the type of institution a student attends.

Kasur Mayor — Education Ranking: 17th → #1 in Punjab

As Chairman (Mayor) of Kasur District Council at just 28 years old — one of the youngest district mayors in Pakistan's history — he oversaw a comprehensive, results-driven approach to public school management. #1 Punjab Education District Mayor Historic Achievement
